## Value Proposition

### The Problem

Every year, thousands of experienced product professionals leave full-time roles to become fractional consultants. They have deep expertise but face three immediate challenges they were never trained for:

1. **No professional brand** -- No published content, no case studies, no public proof of expertise. They go from being "Head of Product at X" to being invisible.
2. **No lead pipeline** -- Their LinkedIn network contains potential clients, but they have no system to identify, qualify, or engage them.
3. **No operational backbone** -- Managing multiple clients with scattered tools (Notion, Google Docs, spreadsheets) leads to missed deadlines and dropped context.

Existing tools solve pieces of this -- Jasper writes generic content, HubSpot manages CRM, Notion organizes docs -- but none of them understand the fractional professional's unique workflow where content creation, lead generation, and client delivery are deeply interconnected.

### The Solution

NextUp is the only platform purpose-built for the full-time-to-fractional transition. It combines four AI agents into one workspace:

**One sentence:** NextUp turns experienced product professionals into thriving fractional consultants by automating their brand building, lead generation, and client operations with AI that knows their voice, their network, and their expertise.

## Main Features

### 1. Portfolio Creation Agent

**What it does:** Automatically produces blog posts, case studies, and social media content that matches your writing voice.

**How it works:**
- Researches your topic from 6 web sources (Reddit, LinkedIn, Quora, Medium, Substack)
- Analyzes 20+ characteristics of your writing style from uploaded samples
- Designs a storytelling structure with narrative arc and emotional journey
- Generates a content skeleton for your approval before writing
- Writes full content section-by-section in your voice
- Removes 24 AI writing patterns via Humanity Check
- Generates images with DALL-E 3 / Gemini Imagen 4
- Delivers publish-ready content

**Why it matters:** Fractional professionals need published thought leadership to attract clients, but writing authentic content takes hours. NextUp produces content that sounds like you wrote it, not like AI did.

### 2. Leads Engine

**What it does:** Transforms your existing LinkedIn network into a qualified lead pipeline.

**How it works:**
- Import LinkedIn connections via CSV export
- 4-layer company classification pipeline (deterministic, LinkedIn lookup, LLM batch, fail-open)
- Hybrid ICP scoring: quantitative formula + qualitative LLM assessment
- Ranks every connection by service relevance
- Generates personalized engagement messages and lead magnets
- Filters out "Not Relevant" contacts automatically

**Why it matters:** New fractionals sit on hundreds of LinkedIn connections without knowing which ones need their services. NextUp turns a static contact list into an active pipeline.

### 3. Customer Management Agent

**What it does:** Manages the full client lifecycle from lead to archive in one AI-powered workspace.

**How it works:**
- 7-stage customer pipeline: Lead, Prospect, Negotiation, Live, On Hold, Archive, Not Relevant
- Service agreements with scope, type, pricing, and date tracking
- Financial receivables: invoices, payments, outstanding balance tracking
- Project management with deliverable artifacts and rich text editor
- AI agent that documents meetings, creates action items, and prepares future meetings
- Event timeline with full interaction history
- Dashboard with aggregate metrics (total customers, active engagements, outstanding revenue, expiring agreements)

**Why it matters:** Fractionals juggle multiple clients across spreadsheets and docs. One missed deadline or forgotten follow-up damages their reputation. NextUp keeps everything in one place with an AI assistant that never forgets.

### 4. Product Management Agent

**What it does:** Provides 60+ AI-powered product management tools to execute client work at scale.

**How it works:**
- Data analysis and product analytics
- Feature prioritization frameworks
- Product design and specification
- User research and interview planning
- Competitive analysis
- MVP scoping and roadmap planning
- UX/UI design guidance
- Growth strategy and launch planning
- Build-vs-buy evaluation
- Persona and ICP creation

**Why it matters:** A fractional handling 3-5 clients needs to context-switch constantly. The Product Management Agent provides the leverage to deliver senior-level work across multiple engagements simultaneously -- the "fractional superpower."

### 5. Guided Onboarding

**What it does:** Gets new users from signup to operational in under 2 minutes.

**How it works:**
- AI extracts professional profile from LinkedIn URL and/or website
- Waterfall reveal of pre-filled fields as extraction completes
- 5-step wizard: Welcome, Profile, Market, Writing References, Completion
- Field provenance system shows what AI extracted vs. what the user edited
- Writing reference upload for immediate voice matching

**Why it matters:** Fractionals are time-poor. A long onboarding kills adoption. NextUp's AI-powered onboarding means you're publishing content and managing clients within minutes of signing up.
