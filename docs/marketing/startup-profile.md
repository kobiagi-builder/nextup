## 1. About the Startup

**Name:** NextUp

**Industry/Vertical:** B2B SaaS - AI-Powered Professional Services Technology (ConsultantTech / CreatorTech)

**Founded:** January 2026

**One-liner:** An AI-native operating system for Fractional product service providers, advisors, and consultants.

**What makes NextUp unique:**
NextUp is built in a period where many product professionals turn from full-time jobs to fractional product service providers.
It's the only product that helps its users make this transition professionally, quickly, and successfully.

---

## 2. Main Product/Service

**Product type:** Web application (SaaS)

**Core features:**

| Feature                   | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Portfolio Creation Agent  | AI-based pipeline that automatically creates the required content for market presence and domain expertise required for the fractional professional in order to build their own professional brand: research, style analysis, storytelling design, skeleton, writing, humanity check, image generation, publish-ready output |
| Leads Engine              | Turns the user's personal LinkedIn profile into a powerful leads engine by automatically analyzing existing connections, ranking their service relevance score, and then creating personalized engagement messages and lead magnets. Every connection can and should become a lead.                                         |
| Customer Management Agent | CRM-lite with lifecycle tracking, agreements, invoicing, projects, and deliverable artifacts. The CRM is built on top of a specialized Customer Management AI agent that documents meetings, creates action items, and prepares future meetings.                                                                          |
| Product Management Agent  | 60+ AI tools to execute customer product management requirements such as data analysis, prioritization, product design and specification, customer interviews, and many other capabilities that provide fractional superpowers to successfully handle many customers in parallel.                                            |

**Target audience:**

- Product consultants and advisors
- Fractional CTOs, CPOs, and CMOs
- Independent strategy consultants
- Agency founders and service providers
- Anyone who needs to publish thought leadership regularly but struggles with time and authenticity

**Main value delivered:**

1. **Guided transition from a full-time job into a fractional product service** -- Provides the required tools and guidance to make this transition quickly, professionally, and successfully.
2. **Leads creation** -- The creation of the leads flywheel is extremely challenging. NextUp does that automatically based on the customer's LinkedIn connections and network.
3. **Time savings** - Produces publish-ready portfolio content such as blog posts, case studies, and social media content in minutes instead of hours
4. **Client visibility** - Manage your entire client pipeline alongside your content in one workspace
5. **Intelligence** -- AI agents that understand your professional context, your clients, and your writing voice to provide increasingly personalized output
